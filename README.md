## 🌐 Overview
The **Neuro-Safety Dashboard** is a self-simulation tool designed to help humans understand the biological limits of AI interaction. While AI can process information at near-infinite speeds, the human brain is bound by a **60-bit per second bottleneck**. 

This tool visualizes the "physics" of cognitive load, metabolic debt, and the risk of **Jacksonian Dissolution**—where high-velocity AI interaction bypasses our critical auditing capacity.

## 🚀 Key Features
- **Dynamic 60-Bit Funnel:** Watch in real-time as your "Effective Capacity" shrinks based on time of day and complexity.
- **Physics-Based Simulation:** Experience how high-complexity outputs (tokens) and frequency (TSF) lead to cognitive "bouncing" (information surprisal).
- **Metabolic Load Tracking:** Visualize the accumulation of lpFC Glutamate and its impact on your "Vagal Brake."
- **Internationalized Safety Protocols:** Localized guidance in **English, French, German, and Spanish**.
- **Theory Integration:** Direct links to the Architecture of Cognitive Governance.

## 🧪 Core Concepts
- **TSF (Task Switching Frequency):** The speed at which you oscillate between different cognitive contexts.
- **CLN (Audit Latency):** The actual time you have to consciously audit an AI output before the next interaction.
- **Metabolic Debt:** The biological tax paid by the prefrontal cortex, which increases as the day progresses.

## 🛠 How to Use
1. **Adjust Frequency:** See how "bursty" usage impacts your $T_b$ (Biological Time).
2. **Select Complexity:** Use presets (Short Answer, Deep Research) to see how volume impacts auditing.
3. **Set Time of Day:** Observe how your biological capacity "throttles" naturally in the evening.
4. **Follow Protocols:** If you hit the **Amber** or **Red** zones, follow the suggested resets to maintain predictive integrity.
